The Challenge

Drowning in Manual Processes That Cost Thousands Every Week

  • Paper-based client intake — New clients filled out physical forms that paralegals manually re-typed into the case management system, averaging 45 minutes per intake
  • No automated follow-up — Potential clients who inquired but didn't retain the firm were never contacted again; an estimated 35% of qualified leads fell through the cracks
  • Manual document preparation — Attorneys and paralegals spent 12+ hours per week drafting repetitive documents (demand letters, motions, correspondence) from scratch
  • Disjointed systems — Separate platforms for email, case management, billing, and calendaring with no integration; staff re-entered the same data 3-4 times across systems
  • Missed deadlines — Calendar and deadline tracking relied on individual attorneys' personal methods, creating compliance risk
The Solution

A Custom Automation System Built in Under 30 Days

  • Digital intake pipeline — Custom web forms auto-populate the CRM, create case files, assign attorneys, and trigger welcome email sequences — intake dropped from 45 minutes to under 5
  • Automated lead nurture — AI-powered follow-up sequences engage prospects at days 1, 3, 7, and 14 with personalized emails, then alert the assigned attorney when a lead re-engages
  • AI document drafting — Template-based system pre-fills case data into demand letters, motions, and correspondence; attorneys review and edit instead of writing from scratch
  • Unified dashboard — Single-pane view connecting case status, billing, calendar deadlines, and client communications across the entire firm
  • Automated deadline tracking — Statute of limitations, filing deadlines, and court dates auto-calculate and push reminders to attorneys 30, 14, and 3 days out
