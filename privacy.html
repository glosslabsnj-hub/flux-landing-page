Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026

Flux Data Solutions ("we," "us," or "our"), a division of Gloss Labs LLC, operates the website getfluxdata.com and provides business automation and AI consulting services. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share your personal information.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and company name when you fill out a contact form, book a call, or subscribe to communications
  • Business information you share during consultations or service delivery
  • Payment information processed through Stripe (we do not store credit card numbers)
  • Communications you send us via email, phone, or text message

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Send appointment reminders and service-related notifications via email or SMS
  • Respond to your inquiries and communicate with you about our services
  • Process payments and send invoices
  • Send marketing communications (with your consent, which you can withdraw at any time)

SMS/Text Messaging

If you provide your phone number and consent to receiving text messages (by booking a call, submitting a contact form, or verbally agreeing), we may send you:

  • Appointment reminders and confirmations
  • Service-related notifications
  • Follow-up messages related to your inquiry

Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Reply HELP for assistance. We will never send unsolicited marketing messages via SMS without your prior express written consent.

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service providers that help us operate our business (Stripe for payments, Google Workspace for email, Twilio for SMS, Calendly for scheduling)
  • As required by law or to protect our rights

Data Security

We use commercially reasonable measures to protect your information, including encrypted communications, secure payment processing through Stripe, and access controls on our systems.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at jack@getfluxdata.com. You can unsubscribe from marketing emails by clicking the unsubscribe link or replying "unsubscribe." You can opt out of SMS by replying STOP.
