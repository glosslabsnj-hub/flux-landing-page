The Challenge

No-Shows, Lost Patients, and Front Desk Burnout

  • 18% no-show rate — Patients regularly missed appointments with no advance warning, leaving $4,200+ in empty chair time per week across all locations
  • Manual reminder calls — Front desk staff spent 3+ hours daily making confirmation calls, pulling them away from in-office patients and scheduling
  • Paper intake forms — New patients filled out 6-page paper forms in the waiting room, then staff manually entered data into the practice management system—averaging 20 minutes per patient
  • No recall system — Patients overdue for cleanings and check-ups were only contacted when staff remembered; an estimated 40% of recall-eligible patients were never reached
  • Zero online reviews — Despite high patient satisfaction scores, the practice had almost no Google or Yelp reviews because nobody thought to ask
The Solution

An Automated Patient Experience System in 3 Weeks

  • Smart appointment reminders — Automated SMS and email reminders at 7 days, 2 days, and 2 hours before each appointment with one-tap confirmation and rescheduling links
  • Digital patient intake — Custom online forms sent automatically 48 hours before appointments, pre-populating practice management software—intake dropped from 20 minutes to under 2
  • Automated recall sequences — Patients overdue for hygiene visits receive personalized outreach at 1 month, 3 months, and 6 months with direct online booking links
  • Post-visit review requests — Automated texts sent 2 hours after appointments asking satisfied patients to leave a Google review, with a direct link to the review page
  • Waitlist management — When cancellations occur, the system automatically texts patients on the waitlist to fill the slot—recovering 70% of cancelled appointments
