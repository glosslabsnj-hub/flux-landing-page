The Challenge

Estimates Disappearing Into a Black Hole

  • 25% close rate on estimates — The owner sent 800+ estimates per year but had no system to follow up; most quotes sat in email or a spreadsheet and were never touched again after the initial send
  • Leads lost in personal inboxes — Inquiries came in via phone, email, website, and Angi/HomeAdvisor, but there was no central system to capture them; the office manager tracked everything in a personal Gmail inbox
  • No-shows on scheduled jobs — Customers regularly forgot about scheduled service appointments, costing the company $1,500+ per week in wasted truck rolls and crew downtime
  • Zero online review strategy — Despite 20+ years and thousands of happy customers, the company had just 23 Google reviews and a 4.1 rating — competitors with half the experience ranked higher in local search
  • Manual scheduling chaos — The office manager spent 2+ hours daily coordinating crew schedules, customer calls, and material pickups across three separate calendars and a whiteboard
The Solution

A Complete Lead-to-Revenue System in 2 Weeks

  • Automated estimate follow-up — Every estimate now gets automatic follow-up at 2 days, 5 days, and 12 days after sending, with personalized messages that reference the specific job and amount — recovering quotes that would have gone cold
  • Unified lead capture — All inquiries from phone, email, website, Angi, and HomeAdvisor automatically funnel into one CRM with instant acknowledgment sent to the customer within 5 minutes
  • Smart appointment reminders — Customers receive SMS and email reminders at 48 hours and 2 hours before scheduled service, with one-tap confirmation and easy rescheduling links
  • Automated review generation — After every completed job, customers receive a text with a direct link to leave a Google review — timed for 2 hours post-completion when satisfaction is highest
  • Centralized scheduling dashboard — One calendar view showing all three crews, customer appointments, material pickups, and permit inspections with automatic conflict detection
