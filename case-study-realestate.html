The Challenge

Leads Falling Through the Cracks While Agents Drowned in Admin Work

  • Leads slipping through the cracks constantly — Inquiries from Zillow, Realtor.com, the brokerage website, and open houses were landing in different inboxes and spreadsheets with no unified system; an estimated 35% of inbound leads never received a single follow-up
  • Painfully slow lead response times — On average, it took 4 hours for an agent to respond to a new property inquiry; by then, buyers had already contacted two or three competing agencies and moved on
  • Manual MLS listing management eating up hours — Agents spent 6–8 hours per week manually entering and updating listings across MLS, Zillow, Realtor.com, and social media; photos were uploaded one portal at a time, and price changes required updating each platform separately
  • 22+ hours per week lost to admin per agent — Between data entry, scheduling showings, sending reminder texts, preparing CMAs, and filing paperwork, agents were spending more time on admin than actually selling homes
  • No system for nurturing long-term prospects — Buyers not ready to purchase for 3–6 months were effectively forgotten; there was no drip campaign, no market updates, and no automated check-ins to keep the agency top of mind
The Solution

AI-Powered Lead Management and Complete Workflow Automation

  • AI-powered lead routing with instant auto-follow-up — All inbound leads from every source funnel into one system; AI instantly categorizes buyer intent, assigns the lead to the best-matched agent by specialty and availability, and fires off a personalized response within 90 seconds—cutting average response time from 4 hours to 12 minutes
  • Automated listing syndication across all platforms — New listings and updates are entered once and automatically pushed to MLS, Zillow, Realtor.com, Redfin, Facebook Marketplace, and Instagram—including photos, descriptions, and pricing changes—eliminating hours of duplicate data entry per listing
  • Smart CRM with intelligent reminders and follow-up sequences — Every lead gets a tailored nurture sequence based on their timeline and preferences; the system automatically sends market updates, new listing alerts, and check-in messages, with smart reminders prompting agents to make personal calls at the right moments
  • Virtual tour and showing scheduling automation — Buyers can self-schedule showings and virtual tours directly from listing pages; the system coordinates agent availability, sends confirmations and reminders to all parties, and syncs everything to Google Calendar—eliminating the back-and-forth scheduling calls
  • Automated CMA and market report generation — Comparative market analyses and neighborhood reports are auto-generated with current data and branded templates, ready for agents to review and send in minutes instead of hours of manual research and formatting
