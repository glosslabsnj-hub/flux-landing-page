SMS Terms & Consent

Last updated: March 22, 2026

Flux Data Solutions (a division of Gloss Labs LLC, "we," "us," or "our") provides the Flux Receptionist platform, an AI-powered phone answering and scheduling service for businesses. This page describes our SMS messaging practices.

How We Collect Consent

We obtain consent to send SMS messages through the following methods:

  • Business Owner Consent (Account Setup): When a business owner signs up for Flux Receptionist at app.getfluxdata.com, they check a clearly labeled checkbox that reads: "I agree to receive automated text messages from Flux Receptionist including appointment confirmations, booking reminders, and system notifications. Message frequency varies. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out at any time."
  • Customer Consent (During Phone Calls): When a customer calls a business that uses Flux Receptionist, our AI receptionist verbally informs the caller: "We will send you appointment reminders and updates via text. Message and data rates may apply. You can reply STOP to opt out at any time. Is that okay?" SMS messages are only sent to customers who provide verbal affirmative consent during the call.

Types of Messages

You may receive the following types of text messages:

  • Appointment confirmations and reminders
  • Booking status updates and scheduling changes
  • Service-related notifications from the business you contacted
  • Follow-up messages after your appointment or service
  • Review requests following completed services

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your interactions. Typically, you will receive 2 to 5 messages per appointment or service interaction (confirmation, reminder, follow-up). Business owners may receive additional system notifications.

Opt-Out

You can opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any text message you receive from us. You will receive a single confirmation message and no further texts will be sent. You may also contact the business directly to request removal from SMS communications.

Opt Back In

To resume receiving messages, reply START to any previous message thread, or contact the business directly to re-enable notifications.

Help

For assistance, reply HELP to any message, or contact Flux Data Solutions at jack@getfluxdata.com or call (609) 731-8641.

Message & Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan. Flux Data Solutions is not responsible for carrier charges.

Privacy

Your phone number is collected solely for the purpose of sending appointment-related and service-related notifications. Your phone number will not be sold to or shared with third parties for marketing purposes. For our full privacy practices, please see our Privacy Policy.

Supported Carriers

SMS messages are supported on all major US carriers including AT&T, Verizon, T-Mobile, Sprint, and most regional carriers.
