The Challenge

Drowning in Phone Calls, No-Shows, and Unmanaged Reviews

  • 18% no-show rate on reservations — Guests regularly booked tables and never showed up, leaving empty seats during peak dinner hours and costing the restaurant an estimated $2,800+ per week in lost revenue
  • Manual reservation management — All reservations were taken by phone or in person and written in a paper book; double-bookings happened weekly, and the host spent 90+ minutes per day fielding calls instead of greeting guests
  • Unmanaged online reviews — The restaurant had a 3.8-star Google rating with dozens of unanswered reviews across Google, Yelp, and TripAdvisor; negative reviews sat for months without a response, driving away potential new customers
  • No inventory visibility — The kitchen regularly ran out of key ingredients mid-service because stock levels were tracked on a clipboard; weekend specials were cancelled 2–3 times per month due to supply shortages
  • Zero customer retention system — No way to track repeat customers, send birthday offers, or follow up after visits; the restaurant relied entirely on word-of-mouth with no email list or loyalty program
The Solution

A Complete Restaurant Automation System in Under 3 Weeks

  • Automated reservation system with SMS confirmations — Online booking integrated with the restaurant’s website and Google Business Profile, with automatic SMS confirmations at booking and reminders at 24 hours and 2 hours before the reservation—reducing no-shows by 67%
  • AI-powered review response system — Monitors Google, Yelp, and TripAdvisor in real time; drafts personalized responses to every review within hours, escalating negative feedback to the owner for a personal touch before publishing
  • Automated inventory alerts — Connected the kitchen’s ordering system with usage tracking so the chef receives automatic alerts when stock on key ingredients drops below reorder thresholds—eliminating mid-service shortages
  • Customer loyalty tracking and personalized marketing — Built a customer database from reservation and POS data, enabling automated birthday emails, anniversary offers, and “we miss you” campaigns for guests who haven’t visited in 60+ days
  • Online ordering and delivery platform management — Unified DoorDash, UberEats, and direct website orders into a single dashboard with automatic menu syncing, pricing updates, and order routing to the kitchen printer
