The Solution

Automation That Fills Chairs and Frees Your Team

We build a custom automation system for your practice. No generic software — everything is tailored to how your office actually runs.

Smart Appointment Reminders

Automated SMS and email reminders at 7 days, 2 days, and 2 hours before appointments. Patients confirm with one tap. If they can't make it, the system offers instant rescheduling — no phone call needed.

67% reduction in no-shows
🔄

Automated Patient Recall System

Patients overdue for hygiene visits get personalized outreach at 1 month, 3 months, and 6 months. Each message includes a direct booking link. No more chasing — they schedule themselves.

94% recall compliance achieved
⭐

Automated Review Requests

Two hours after every appointment, satisfied patients receive a text with a direct link to leave a Google review. Unhappy patients are routed to a private feedback form so you can fix it first.

4.9 Google rating with 203 reviews

Insurance Pre-Verification

Insurance eligibility is automatically checked 48 hours before each appointment. Your team gets a clear report — covered vs. not covered, co-pay amounts, remaining benefits — before the patient walks in.

3+ hours saved daily on verifications

Waitlist Management

When a patient cancels, the system automatically texts your waitlist patients to fill the slot. First to reply gets the appointment. No manual calling, no empty chairs. Practices using this recover 70% of last-minute cancellations.
